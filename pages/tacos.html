Tacos!

Description

These tacos are great for dinner!

Ingredients

  • Minced beef
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Tortilla

Steps

  1. Grate cheese
  2. Dice lettuce & tomato
  3. Cook minced beef until browned
  4. Serve on tortilla
  5. Top with salt & pepper

Conclusion

These steps are simple, easy and produce great tacos!
