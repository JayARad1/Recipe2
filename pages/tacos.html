Tacos!

Description

These tacos are great for dinner!

Ingredients

  • Minced beef
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Tortilla

    • Steps

  • Grate cheese
  • Dice lettuce & tomato
  • Cook minced beef until browned
  • Serve on tortilla
  • Top with salt & pepper

    • Conclusion

    These steps are simple, easy and produce great tacos!
