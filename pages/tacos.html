Tacos!

Description

These tacos are great for dinner!

Ingredients

  • Minced beef
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Tortilla

    • Steps

  • Grate cheese.
  • Dice lettuce and tomato.
  • Cook minced beef until browned.
  • Serve on a tortilla.
  • Top with salt and pepper.

    • Conclusion

    These steps are simple, easy, and produce great tacos!
