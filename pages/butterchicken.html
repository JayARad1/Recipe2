Butterchicken!

Description

This butterchicken are great for dinner!

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs
  • Butter chicken spices
  • Garlic naan bread
  • Rice

    • Steps

  • Cook chicken in a pan.
  • Boil rice in a pot.
  • Heat garlic naan bread.
  • Once cooked, add butter chicken spices to the pan and stir, allow to simmer on low heat.
  • Drain rice and add to serving bowl.
  • Complete heating garlic naan bread and add to a seperate small plate.
  • Top rice with butter chicken.

    • Conclusion

    These steps are fairly simple, easy and produces amazing butter chicken!
