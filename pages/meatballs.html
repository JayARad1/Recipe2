Meatballs!

Description

These meatballs are great for dinner!

Ingredients

  • Minced beef
  • Spaghetti
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Tomato base sauce

    • Steps

  • Finely grate parmesan cheese.
  • Ball up minced meat into balls & bake for 35 minutes. Start boiling the spaghetti pasta.
  • Preheat tomato base sauce once pasta & meatballs are done & add meatballs to sauce.
  • Once meatballs are covered adequately, drain spaghetti and add to serving bowl.
  • Top spaghetti with the meatballs, then top meatballs with parmesan cheese.

    • Conclusion

    These steps are fairly simple, easy and produce great meatballs if done right!
