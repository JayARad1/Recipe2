Meatballs!

Description

These meatballs are great for dinner!

Ingredients

  • Minced beef
  • Spaghetti
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Tomato base sauce

    • Steps

  • Finely grate parmesan cheese.
  • Form minced meat into balls and bake for 35 minutes. Start boiling the spaghetti pasta.
  • Heat the tomato base sauce once the pasta and meatballs are done, then add the meatballs to the sauce.
  • Once the meatballs are adequately covered, drain the spaghetti and add it to a serving bowl.
  • Top the spaghetti with the meatballs, then sprinkle parmesan cheese over the meatballs.

    • Conclusion

    These steps are fairly simple, easy, and produce great meatballs if done right!
