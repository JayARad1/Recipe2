Meatballs!

Description

These meatballs are great for dinner!

Ingredients

  • Minced beef
  • Spaghetti
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Tomato base sauce

Steps

  1. Finely grate parmesan cheese.
  2. Ball up minced meat into balls & bake for 35 minutes. Start boiling the spaghetti pasta.
  3. Preheat tomato base sauce once pasta & meatballs are done & add meatballs to sauce.
  4. Once meatballs are covered adequately, drain spaghetti and add to serving bowl.
  5. Top spaghetti with the meatballs, then top meatballs with parmesan cheese.

Conclusion

These steps are fairly simple, easy and produce great meatballs if done right!
